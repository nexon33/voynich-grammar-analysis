```

oak-DEF   vessel  mix-VERB      oak-bark-INST   stir-VERB    place-VERB?
```

**Translation:**
```
"The oak, [in] vessel, mix with oak bark, stir, [apply to] place"
```

**Medieval parallel (Hildegard of Bingen):**
